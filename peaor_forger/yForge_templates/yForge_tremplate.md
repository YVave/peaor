# Idea Title

## Purpose
- **Description**: Describe the purpose of the idea.
- **Intent**: Explain the intention behind the idea.

## Energy
- **Resources**: List the resources required.
- **Focus**: Describe the focus of energy needed.

## Action
- **Steps**: Outline the steps to implement the idea.
- **Timeline**: Provide a timeline for the actions.

## Output
- **Expected Results**: Describe the expected outcomes.
- **Impact**: Explain the potential impact of the idea.

## Resonance
- **Feedback Mechanism**: Outline how feedback will be collected.
- **Adaptation**: Describe how the idea will adapt based on feedback.

## Metrics
- **Evaluation Criteria**: List the criteria for evaluating the success of the idea.
- **Data Collection**: Describe how data will be collected and analyzed.
